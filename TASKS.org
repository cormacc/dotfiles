Follow-up to the keybindings refactor (parent task 00e1772c, change-record design/log/2026-05-02-refactor-keybindings-extension.org).

vim-mode/index.ts is a ~2200-line monolith carrying the VimEditor class, all motion / operator / text-object helpers, dot-repeat recording, and the entry point. Mirror the upstream `kepatrick/vim-motions-pi` decomposition shape:

- core.ts — pure motion math (clamp, isWordChar, isWhitespace, isSymbol, comparePos, minPos, maxPos, position arithmetic).
- buffer.ts — text-manipulation primitives (line ops, indent/dedent, region helpers).
- index.ts — entry point + VimEditor class. Keep vim-mode's single-responsibility shape: no slash command, settings file, or settings migrator.

Pure refactor — behaviour must not change. Captured during the keybindings split when the KEEP_OWN evaluation noted upstream's cleaner factoring.
